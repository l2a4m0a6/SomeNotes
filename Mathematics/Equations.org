** Arithmetic Progression

a_{n} = a + (n-1)d

S_{n} = $\frac{n}{2} \left( 2a + (n-1)d \right)$

a_{n} = a r^{n-1}

S = a $\left( \frac{1 - r^n}{1-r}\right)$

H_{n} = $\frac{1}{a + (n-1)d}$

A.M. = $\frac{S}{n}$

G.M. = $\sqrt[3]{a_1 \times a_2 \times a_n}$

H.M. = $\frac{2ab}{a + b}$

A.M X H.M = G.M^{2}
